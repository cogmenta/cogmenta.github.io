The Problem

The most productive expert work happens in the firms that cannot adopt cloud AI.

Trillion-dollar professional-services markets — finance, law, healthcare, audit — run on data that legally cannot leave the firm. Three structural barriers keep the current AI wave outside the firewall.

Data residency

Deal memos, client files, privileged work product cannot cross the firewall. Cloud APIs are legally unusable; jurisdictions stack data-localization rules on top of sector regulation.

Audit trail

Regulators require traceable, explainable decisions. Black-box outputs fail audit. In finance and healthcare, explainability is a legal requirement — not a feature.

Firm-specific judgment

Expert firms run on proprietary house style, prior decisions, and internal records. Retrieval-only systems get partway; the last mile — where judgment lives — is where general AI fails.
