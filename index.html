The Problem

The most valuable work happens where cloud AI cannot reach.

Whether the work is regulated, privileged, proprietary, or simply physical — the most productive workflows in the economy stay behind the firewall. Three structural barriers keep the current AI wave out.

Data residency

Deal memos, patient records, trade secrets, operating data — none of it can cross the firewall. Public AI APIs are off the table; data-localization rules add another layer on top.

Audit trail

Regulators, auditors, and safety reviewers require traceable, explainable decisions. Black-box outputs fail audit. Explainability is a legal and operational requirement, not a feature.

Workflow-specific judgment

Every company runs on proprietary processes, decisions, and tacit knowledge. Retrieval-only systems get partway; the last mile — where judgment lives — is where generic AI fails.
